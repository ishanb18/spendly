Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal information you provide when creating an account, including your name, email address, and password (stored securely). When you use the expense‑tracking features, we collect the expense data you enter, such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also automatically collect basic usage metrics—such as login times and feature interactions—to help us improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide to operate and personalize Spendly, including displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and supporting core functionality. Aggregated, de‑identified usage data may be used to analyze trends and enhance the product. We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use your expense information for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers using industry‑standard encryption in transit and at rest. We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from active databases within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use trusted third‑party providers to host the application, send transactional emails, or perform analytics. These providers receive only the data necessary to deliver their services and are contractually obligated to protect your information. We do not share your expense details with advertisers or marketing partners.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, your data, or your account, please contact us at support@spendly.app. We will respond to inquiries as promptly as possible.
